Class 10 Maths Previous Year Questions (2024) - Constructions

  1. Construct a triangle ABC with BC = 7 cm, angle B = 60°, and AB + AC = 10 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Draw BC = 7 cm. At B, draw ray BX making 60° with BC. Cut BD = 5 cm on BX (half of 10 cm). Join DC. Draw perpendicular bisector of BD intersecting BC at A. Join AB and AC. Verify AB + AC = 10 cm.

  2. Draw a line segment AB = 8 cm. Divide it in the ratio 3:2 internally. (3 marks)

    Answer: Draw AB = 8 cm. Draw ray AX. Mark 5 points (3+2) on AX at equal distances. Join last point to B. Draw parallel from 3rd point to AB, intersecting AB at P. AP:PB = 3:2.

  3. Construct a triangle similar to given triangle ABC with scale factor 3/4. (4 marks)

    Answer: Draw ΔABC. Extend BC to D such that CD = (1/4)BC. Draw perpendicular bisector of CD intersecting extension at P. Join PA intersecting BC at A'. Draw B'C' parallel to AB. ΔA'B'C' ~ ΔABC, scale 3/4.

  4. Draw a tangent to a circle of radius 4 cm from a point 6 cm away from center. (3 marks)

    Answer: Draw circle with center O, radius 4 cm. Mark point P, 6 cm from O. Bisect OP at M. Draw circle with center M, radius MP. Intersect with first circle at A,B. Join PA, PB (tangents).

  5. Construct ΔABC with AB = 5 cm, AC = 6 cm, and median from A to BC = 5.5 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Draw AB = 5 cm. At A, draw arc radius 6 cm for C. Midpoint M of BC. Use median length to locate C such that AM = 5.5 cm. Join BC.

  6. Divide line segment PQ = 9 cm in ratio 2:3 externally. (3 marks)

    Answer: Draw PQ = 9 cm. Ray PX opposite to Q. Mark 5 points (2+3). Join 5th to Q. Parallel from 2nd point to PQ intersecting extension at R. PR:RQ = 2:3 externally.

  7. Construct a pair of tangents to a circle from an external point. (4 marks)

    Answer: Circle center O, radius r. Point P outside. Join OP. Midpoint M of OP. Circle center M, radius MP intersecting original at A,B. PA, PB tangents.

  8. Draw a triangle with sides 5 cm, 6 cm, 7 cm and construct its incircle. (4 marks)

    Answer: Construct ΔABC with sides 5,6,7 cm. Find incenter I by angle bisectors intersection. Draw perpendiculars from I to sides, radius to touch points.

  9. Construct an equilateral triangle on base BC = 6 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Draw BC = 6 cm. Arcs from B and C radius 6 cm intersect at A. Join AB, AC. ΔABC equilateral.

  10. Divide AB = 10 cm in ratio 4:1 internally. (3 marks)

    Answer: Draw AB = 10 cm. Ray AX, 5 equal parts. Join 5th to B. Parallel from 4th to AB at P. AP:PB = 4:1.

  11. Construct a right triangle with hypotenuse 8 cm and one leg 6 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Draw leg AB = 6 cm. At B, 90° angle. Arc radius 8 cm from A intersecting ray at C. Join BC.

  12. Draw a circle touching x-axis at (3,0) and passing through (0,3). (4 marks)

    Answer: Center on perpendicular to x-axis at (3,0), i.e., x=3. Radius = y-coordinate. Passes through (0,3): distance from (3,r) to (0,3) = r. Solve: r = 3/2 or 6. Select appropriate.

  13. Construct ΔPQR similar to ΔABC with PQ = 4 cm, scale 2/3. (3 marks)

    Answer: Draw PQ = 4 cm. Angle at P same as A. Arc for side proportional. Parallel line for similarity.

  14. Find point dividing join of (1,2) and (4,6) in 1:1. (3 marks)

    Answer: Midpoint: ((1+4)/2, (2+6)/2) = (2.5, 4). Construct using section formula geometrically.

  15. Construct tangent from point to circle of radius 5 cm, distance 13 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Pythagoras: tangent length √(13²-5²)=12 cm. Use construction method with perpendicular bisector.

  16. Draw rhombus with diagonals 6 cm and 8 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Draw diagonals intersecting at O, half lengths. Join vertices perpendicular.

  17. Construct angle bisector in triangle with sides 5,5,6 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Isosceles triangle. Bisector from apex to base midpoint.

  18. Divide line in 3:5 ratio externally. (3 marks)

    Answer: Similar to internal but opposite ray, parallel from appropriate point.

  19. Construct square with side 5 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Draw side, perpendiculars, arcs for equal sides.

  20. Draw circle with diameter AB = 6 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Midpoint O, radius 3 cm, draw circle.

  21. Construct parallelogram with sides 4 cm, 5 cm, angle 60°. (4 marks)

    Answer: Draw side 4 cm, angle 60°, parallel lines for opposite sides.

  22. Find point dividing in 1:3 internally. (3 marks)

    Answer: Section formula, construct using ratios.

  23. Construct isosceles right triangle with legs 5 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Draw leg, 90° at vertex, equal arcs for hypotenuse.

  24. Draw perpendicular bisector of 7 cm segment. (3 marks)

    Answer: Midpoint, set compass to half, arcs intersecting for line.

  25. Construct triangle with angles 50°, 60°, 70°. (4 marks)

    Answer: Draw base, construct angles at ends, intersect arcs.

  26. Divide segment in 1:2 ratio. (3 marks)

    Answer: Thales' theorem with parallels.

  27. Construct circumcircle of equilateral triangle side 4 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Perpendicular bisectors intersect at circumcenter, radius to vertex.

  28. Draw tangent at point on circle. (3 marks)

    Answer: Radius perpendicular to tangent at point.

  29. Construct rectangle with sides 3 cm, 4 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Perpendiculars at 90° for sides.

  30. Find external division in 2:1. (4 marks)

    Answer: External ratio construction using opposite direction.

  31. Construct 30° angle. (3 marks)

    Answer: 60° bisect to 30°.

  32. Draw incircle of triangle with sides 3,4,5 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Angle bisectors for incenter, perpendiculars to sides.

  33. Divide in 5:3 ratio internally. (3 marks)

    Answer: 8 parts, parallel from 5th.

  34. Construct similar triangle scale 1/2. (4 marks)

    Answer: Midpoints or parallel lines half size.

  35. Draw circle through 3 points. (3 marks)

    Answer: Perpendicular bisectors intersection as center.

  36. Construct 45° angle. (3 marks)

    Answer: 90° bisect.

  37. External tangents to two circles. (4 marks)

    Answer: Centers line, similar triangles for points.

  38. Divide segment externally 1:4. (3 marks)

    Answer: Ratio construction external.

  39. Construct trapezium with non-parallel sides 5 cm, bases 4 cm, 6 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: Draw bases, connect with equal non-parallel sides.
