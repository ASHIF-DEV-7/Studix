Class 10 Mathematics Formulas

Circle

Area of Circle:
A = πr²
Circumference:
C = 2πr
Area of Sector:
A = (θ/360°) × πr²
Length of Arc:
l = (θ/360°) × 2πr
Area of Segment:
A = Area of Sector - Area of Triangle

Sphere

Volume:
V = (4/3)πr³
Surface Area:
SA = 4πr²
Volume (diameter):
V = (π/6)d³
Surface Area (diameter):
SA = πd²

Hemisphere

Volume:
V = (2/3)πr³
Curved Surface Area:
CSA = 2πr²
Total Surface Area:
TSA = 3πr²

Cylinder

Volume:
V = πr²h
Curved Surface Area:
CSA = 2πrh
Total Surface Area:
TSA = 2πr(r + h)

Cone

Volume:
V = (1/3)πr²h
Curved Surface Area:
CSA = πrl
Total Surface Area:
TSA = πr(r + l)
Slant Height:
l = √(r² + h²)

Frustum of Cone

Volume:
V = (1/3)πh(r₁² + r₂² + r₁r₂)
Curved Surface Area:
CSA = π(r₁ + r₂)l
Total Surface Area:
TSA = π(r₁² + r₂² + (r₁ + r₂)l)
Slant Height:
l = √(h² + (r₁ - r₂)²)

Symbol Legend

r = radius
d = diameter
h = height
l = slant height
θ = angle in degrees
π = 3.14159...
r₁, r₂ = radii of frustum
