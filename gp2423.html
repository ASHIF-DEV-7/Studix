Class 10 Maths Previous Year Questions (2024) - Triangles

  1. State the Basic Proportionality Theorem (BPT). (3 marks)

    Answer: If a line is drawn parallel to one side of a triangle intersecting the other two sides, it divides them proportionally: DE || BC in ΔABC, then AD/DB = AE/EC.

  2. In ΔABC, DE || BC, AD = 3 cm, DB = 2 cm, AE = 4.5 cm. Find EC. (3 marks)

    Answer: By BPT, AD/DB = AE/EC. 3/2 = 4.5/EC. EC = 4.5 × 2/3 = 3 cm.

  3. Prove that the ratio of areas of similar triangles is equal to the square of their corresponding sides. (4 marks)

    Answer: For ΔABC ~ ΔDEF, ar(ABC)/ar(DEF) = (AB/DE)². Use similarity: equal angles, proportional sides, and area formula (1/2 × base × height).

  4. In ΔPQR, PQ = 6 cm, QR = 8 cm, PR = 10 cm. Is it a right triangle? (3 marks)

    Answer: Check Pythagoras: PR² = 10² = 100, PQ² + QR² = 6² + 8² = 36 + 64 = 100. Yes, right triangle.

  5. State and prove the Pythagoras theorem. (4 marks)

    Answer: In a right triangle, square of hypotenuse equals sum of squares of other sides. Proof: Construct squares on sides, use area equality via similar triangles.

  6. In ΔABC, ∠B = 90°, AB = 8 cm, BC = 6 cm. Find AC. (3 marks)

    Answer: By Pythagoras, AC² = AB² + BC² = 8² + 6² = 64 + 36 = 100. AC = 10 cm.

  7. State the criteria for similarity of triangles. (3 marks)

    Answer: AA (two angles equal), SSS (three sides proportional), SAS (two sides proportional, included angle equal).

  8. In ΔABC and ΔDEF, ∠A = ∠D, ∠B = ∠E, AB/DE = 3/4. Are they similar? (3 marks)

    Answer: By AA criterion (∠A = ∠D, ∠B = ∠E), ΔABC ~ ΔDEF.

  9. In ΔABC, DE || BC, AD/DB = 2/3, AE = 4 cm. Find EC. (3 marks)

    Answer: By BPT, AD/DB = AE/EC. 2/3 = 4/EC. EC = 4 × 3/2 = 6 cm.

  10. Prove that in a right triangle, the square of the hypotenuse equals the sum of squares of other sides. (4 marks)

    Answer: Use Pythagoras: Construct perpendicular from vertex to hypotenuse, forming similar triangles, equate areas.

  11. In ΔABC, ∠C = 90°, AC = 5 cm, BC = 12 cm. Find AB. (3 marks)

    Answer: By Pythagoras, AB² = AC² + BC² = 5² + 12² = 25 + 144 = 169. AB = 13 cm.

  12. If ΔABC ~ ΔPQR, AB/PQ = 2/3, ar(ABC) = 16 cm², find ar(PQR). (4 marks)

    Answer: ar(ABC)/ar(PQR) = (AB/PQ)² = (2/3)² = 4/9. 16/ar(PQR) = 4/9. ar(PQR) = 16 × 9/4 = 36 cm².

  13. In ΔABC, DE || BC, AD = 4 cm, DB = 6 cm, AE = 6 cm. Find EC. (3 marks)

    Answer: By BPT, AD/DB = AE/EC. 4/6 = 6/EC. EC = 6 × 6/4 = 9 cm.

  14. State the converse of the Basic Proportionality Theorem. (3 marks)

    Answer: If a line divides two sides of a triangle proportionally, it is parallel to the third side.

  15. In ΔABC, ∠B = 90°, AB = 3 cm, AC = 5 cm. Find BC. (3 marks)

    Answer: By Pythagoras, AC² = AB² + BC². 5² = 3² + BC². 25 = 9 + BC². BC = √16 = 4 cm.

  16. Prove that if triangles are similar, their corresponding sides are proportional. (4 marks)

    Answer: Use AA similarity, construct parallel lines, apply BPT to show side ratios equal.

  17. In ΔABC, DE || BC, AD = 2 cm, DB = 3 cm, area ratio = 4/9. Find AE/EC. (4 marks)

    Answer: By BPT, AD/DB = AE/EC = 2/3. Area ratio = (AD/DB)² = (2/3)² = 4/9 (verified).

  18. Check if sides 7 cm, 24 cm, 25 cm form a right triangle. (3 marks)

    Answer: 25² = 625, 7² + 24² = 49 + 576 = 625. Right triangle.

  19. In ΔABC, ∠A = 50°, ∠B = 70°. Find ∠C. (3 marks)

    Answer: ∠A + ∠B + ∠C = 180°. 50° + 70° + ∠C = 180°. ∠C = 60°.

  20. Prove that the areas of similar triangles are proportional to the squares of corresponding medians. (4 marks)

    Answer: Similar triangles have proportional sides. Medians proportional to sides, so area ratio = (median ratio)².

  21. In ΔABC, DE || BC, AD = 5 cm, AB = 8 cm. Find AD/DB. (3 marks)

    Answer: AB = AD + DB. 8 = 5 + DB. DB = 3 cm. AD/DB = 5/3.

  22. If ΔABC ~ ΔDEF, AB = 9 cm, DE = 12 cm, find ratio of their areas. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area ratio = (AB/DE)² = (9/12)² = (3/4)² = 9/16.

  23. In a right triangle, hypotenuse is 17 cm, one leg 8 cm. Find the other leg. (3 marks)

    Answer: By Pythagoras, 17² = 8² + x². 289 = 64 + x². x = √225 = 15 cm.

  24. Prove the converse of Pythagoras theorem. (4 marks)

    Answer: If a² + b² = c², triangle is right-angled. Construct right triangle, compare side ratios.

  25. In ΔABC, DE || BC, area of ΔADE = 25 cm², area of ΔABC = 100 cm². Find AD/DB. (4 marks)

    Answer: Area ratio = (AD/AB)². 25/100 = (AD/AB)². AD/AB = 1/2. AB = AD + DB, so AD/DB = 1/1.

  26. In ΔPQR, ∠Q = 90°, PQ = 9 cm, QR = 12 cm. Find PR. (3 marks)

    Answer: By Pythagoras, PR² = PQ² + QR² = 9² + 12² = 81 + 144 = 225. PR = 15 cm.

  27. State the AA similarity criterion. (3 marks)

    Answer: If two angles of one triangle equal two angles of another, the triangles are similar.

  28. In ΔABC, DE || BC, AD = 6 cm, AB = 10 cm, AE = 9 cm. Find EC. (3 marks)

    Answer: By BPT, AD/DB = AE/EC. DB = 10 - 6 = 4 cm. 6/4 = 9/EC. EC = 9 × 4/6 = 6 cm.

  29. Prove that medians of similar triangles are proportional to corresponding sides. (4 marks)

    Answer: Use similarity and mid-point theorem: median divides side, proportional to corresponding sides.

  30. In ΔABC, ∠B = 90°, AB = 12 cm, BC = 16 cm. Find area. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = (1/2) × base × height = (1/2) × 16 × 12 = 96 cm².

  31. If ΔABC ~ ΔDEF, BC = 15 cm, EF = 10 cm, find ratio of areas. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area ratio = (BC/EF)² = (15/10)² = 9/4.

  32. In ΔABC, DE || BC, AD/DB = 3/5, AE = 6 cm. Find EC. (3 marks)

    Answer: By BPT, AD/DB = AE/EC. 3/5 = 6/EC. EC = 6 × 5/3 = 10 cm.

  33. Prove that in similar triangles, corresponding altitudes are proportional. (4 marks)

    Answer: Use similarity: altitudes form right triangles, proportional to corresponding sides.

  34. Check if sides 9 cm, 12 cm, 15 cm form a right triangle. (3 marks)

    Answer: 15² = 225, 9² + 12² = 81 + 144 = 225. Right triangle.

  35. In ΔABC, DE || BC, ar(ΔADE)/ar(ΔABC) = 16/25. Find AD/AB. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area ratio = (AD/AB)². 16/25 = (AD/AB)². AD/AB = 4/5.

  36. In ΔPQR, PQ = 5 cm, QR = 12 cm, PR = 13 cm. Is it a right triangle? (3 marks)

    Answer: 13² = 169, 5² + 12² = 25 + 144 = 169. Right triangle.

  37. Prove that if a line is parallel to one side, it divides others proportionally. (4 marks)

    Answer: Use BPT: Construct triangles, prove similarity, equate ratios.

  38. In ΔABC, ∠C = 90°, AC = 7 cm, BC = 24 cm. Find AB. (3 marks)

    Answer: By Pythagoras, AB² = 7² + 24² = 49 + 576 = 625. AB = 25 cm.

  39. If ΔABC ~ ΔPQR, AB/PQ = 4/5, PQ = 10 cm, find AB. (3 marks)

    Answer: AB/PQ = 4/5. AB/10 = 4/5. AB = 8 cm.

  40. In ΔABC, DE || BC, AD = 7 cm, AB = 14 cm. Find AE/EC. (3 marks)

    Answer: DB = 14 - 7 = 7 cm. By BPT, AD/DB = AE/EC = 7/7 = 1/1.
