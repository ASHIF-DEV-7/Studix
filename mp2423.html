Class 10 Maths Previous Year Questions (2024) - Mensuration

  1. Find the area of a triangle with base 8 cm and height 5 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = (1/2) × base × height = (1/2) × 8 × 5 = 20 cm².

  2. Calculate the perimeter of a rectangle with length 12 cm and breadth 7 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Perimeter = 2(length + breadth) = 2(12 + 7) = 38 cm.

  3. Find the area of a circle with radius 7 cm. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = πr² = (22/7) × 7² = 22 × 7 = 154 cm².

  4. A rectangle has an area of 48 cm² and length 12 cm. Find its breadth. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = length × breadth. 48 = 12 × b. b = 48/12 = 4 cm.

  5. Find the volume of a cube with side 6 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Volume = side³ = 6³ = 216 cm³.

  6. Calculate the surface area of a cube with side 5 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Surface area = 6 × side² = 6 × 5² = 150 cm².

  7. Find the volume of a cylinder with radius 4 cm and height 10 cm. (Use π = 3.14) (4 marks)

    Answer: Volume = πr²h = 3.14 × 4² × 10 = 3.14 × 16 × 10 = 502.4 cm³.

  8. Find the curved surface area of a cylinder with radius 7 cm and height 10 cm. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: CSA = 2πrh = 2 × (22/7) × 7 × 10 = 440 cm².

  9. A cone has radius 3 cm and height 4 cm. Find its volume. (Use π = 3.14) (3 marks)

    Answer: Volume = (1/3)πr²h = (1/3) × 3.14 × 3² × 4 = 37.68 cm³.

  10. Find the total surface area of a cone with radius 5 cm and slant height 13 cm. (Use π = 22/7) (4 marks)

    Answer: TSA = πr(l + r) = (22/7) × 5 × (13 + 5) = (22/7) × 5 × 18 = 282.86 cm².

  11. Calculate the volume of a sphere with radius 6 cm. (Use π = 3.14) (3 marks)

    Answer: Volume = (4/3)πr³ = (4/3) × 3.14 × 6³ = 904.32 cm³.

  12. Find the surface area of a sphere with radius 7 cm. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Surface area = 4πr² = 4 × (22/7) × 7² = 616 cm².

  13. A cuboid has dimensions 10 cm, 6 cm, 5 cm. Find its volume. (3 marks)

    Answer: Volume = l × b × h = 10 × 6 × 5 = 300 cm³.

  14. Find the total surface area of a cuboid with dimensions 8 cm, 6 cm, 4 cm. (4 marks)

    Answer: TSA = 2(lb + bh + hl) = 2(8×6 + 6×4 + 4×8) = 2(48 + 24 + 32) = 208 cm².

  15. A room is 5 m long, 4 m wide, 3 m high. Find the cost of painting its walls at ₹10/m². (4 marks)

    Answer: Area of 4 walls = 2(l + b)h = 2(5 + 4) × 3 = 54 m². Cost = 54 × 10 = ₹540.

  16. Find the area of a parallelogram with base 10 cm and height 6 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = base × height = 10 × 6 = 60 cm².

  17. A trapezium has parallel sides 10 cm, 6 cm, and height 5 cm. Find its area. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = (1/2) × (sum of parallel sides) × height = (1/2) × (10 + 6) × 5 = 40 cm².

  18. Find the circumference of a circle with diameter 14 cm. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Circumference = πd = (22/7) × 14 = 44 cm.

  19. A cylinder has a base area of 154 cm² and height 10 cm. Find its volume. (4 marks)

    Answer: Volume = base area × height = 154 × 10 = 1540 cm³.

  20. Find the slant height of a cone with radius 7 cm and height 24 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Slant height = √(r² + h²) = √(7² + 24²) = √(49 + 576) = 25 cm.

  21. A hemispherical bowl has radius 7 cm. Find its volume. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Volume = (2/3)πr³ = (2/3) × (22/7) × 7³ = 718.67 cm³.

  22. Find the curved surface area of a hemisphere with radius 5 cm. (Use π = 3.14) (3 marks)

    Answer: CSA = 2πr² = 2 × 3.14 × 5² = 157 cm².

  23. A cone and cylinder have equal bases (radius 5 cm) and heights 10 cm. Compare their volumes. (4 marks)

    Answer: Cylinder volume = π × 5² × 10 = 250π cm³. Cone volume = (1/3)π × 5² × 10 = 83.33π cm³. Ratio = 3:1.

  24. A cube has a surface area of 150 cm². Find its side length. (3 marks)

    Answer: 6a² = 150, a² = 25, a = 5 cm.

  25. Find the area of a sector with radius 14 cm and angle 60°. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = (θ/360) × πr² = (60/360) × (22/7) × 14² = 102.67 cm².

  26. A cylindrical tank has radius 1.4 m and height 2 m. Find water volume in liters. (4 marks)

    Answer: Volume = πr²h = (22/7) × 1.4² × 2 = 12.32 m³ = 12320 liters (1 m³ = 1000 liters).

  27. Find the area of a rhombus with diagonals 8 cm and 6 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = (1/2) × d₁ × d₂ = (1/2) × 8 × 6 = 24 cm².

  28. A sphere and cube have equal surface areas. If cube side is 6 cm, find sphere radius. (4 marks)

    Answer: Cube SA = 6 × 6² = 216 cm². Sphere SA = 4πr² = 216. r² = 216/(4 × 3.14) ≈ 17.2, r ≈ 4.15 cm.

  29. Find the volume of a cone with diameter 14 cm and height 24 cm. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Volume = (1/3)πr²h = (1/3) × (22/7) × 7² × 24 = 1232 cm³.

  30. A rectangular park is 50 m by 30 m. Find cost of fencing at ₹15/m. (3 marks)

    Answer: Perimeter = 2(50 + 30) = 160 m. Cost = 160 × 15 = ₹2400.

  31. Find the volume of a frustum with radii 10 cm, 5 cm, and height 6 cm. (Use π = 3.14) (4 marks)

    Answer: Volume = (1/3)πh(R² + r² + Rr) = (1/3) × 3.14 × 6 × (10² + 5² + 10×5) = 2198 cm³.

  32. A cylinder is filled with 3080 cm³ of water. If radius is 7 cm, find height. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Volume = πr²h. 3080 = (22/7) × 7² × h. h = 3080/154 = 20 cm.

  33. Find the area of an equilateral triangle with side 8 cm. (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = (√3/4) × side² = (√3/4) × 8² = 16√3 cm² ≈ 27.71 cm².

  34. A hemispherical dome has radius 10 m. Find cost of painting at ₹20/m². (Use π = 3.14) (4 marks)

    Answer: CSA = 2πr² = 2 × 3.14 × 10² = 628 m². Cost = 628 × 20 = ₹12560.

  35. Find the length of an arc with radius 21 cm and angle 120°. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Arc length = (θ/360) × 2πr = (120/360) × 2 × (22/7) × 21 = 44 cm.

  36. A cuboidal tank is 2 m × 1.5 m × 1 m. Find water capacity in liters. (4 marks)

    Answer: Volume = 2 × 1.5 × 1 = 3 m³ = 3000 liters.

  37. Find the area of a quadrant of a circle with radius 14 cm. (Use π = 22/7) (3 marks)

    Answer: Area = (1/4)πr² = (1/4) × (22/7) × 14² = 154 cm².

  38. A cone is cut 4 cm from base, radii 6 cm and 3 cm, height 12 cm. Find frustum volume. (4 marks)

    Answer: Frustum height = 4 cm. Volume = (1/3)πh(R² + r² + Rr) = (1/3) × 3.14 × 4 × (6² + 3² + 6×3) = 251.2 cm³.

  39. Find the total surface area of a cylinder with diameter 10 cm and height 14 cm. (Use π = 22/7) (4 marks)

    Answer: TSA = 2πr(h + r) = 2 × (22/7) × 5 × (14 + 5) = 597.14 cm².

  40. A square and circle have equal perimeters. Square side is 11 cm. Find circle’s radius. (3 marks)

    Answer: Square perimeter = 4 × 11 = 44 cm. Circle circumference = 2πr = 44. r = 44/(2 × 22/7) = 7 cm.
