Class 10 Mathematics - PYQs (2023)

Q1: Find the value of k for which the quadratic equation 2x² + kx + 3 = 0 has equal roots.

A: k = ±√24

Q2: If the mean of the following data is 20, find the missing frequency f: 10, 15, f, 25, 30.

A: f = 20

Q3: Prove that the tangents drawn at the ends of a chord of a circle are parallel.

A: Proof involves using alternate segment theorem and properties of tangents.

Q4: A solid hemisphere of radius 7 cm is melted and recast into a cone of height 14 cm. Find the radius of the cone.

A: Radius of the cone is 7 cm.

Q5: Solve for x: 2cos²x - 3sinx = 0, where 0° ≤ x ≤ 90°.

A: x = 30°

Q6: Find the mode of the following data: 5, 8, 10, 10, 12, 15, 15, 15, 18, 20.

A: Mode = 15

Q7: In a right-angled triangle, if one angle is 30°, find the other two angles.

A: Other angles are 60° and 90°.

Q8: Find the area of a sector of a circle with radius 14 cm and angle 60°.

A: Area of the sector = 38.48 cm².

Q9: Find the probability of drawing a red card from a deck of 52 cards.

A: Probability = 26/52 = 1/2.

Q10: If the sum of the first n terms of an arithmetic progression is given by Sn = 3n² + 2n, find the nth term.

A: nth term = 6n + 2.

Q11: Find the distance between the points (3, -2) and (7, 4).

A: Distance = 5 units.

Q12: Prove that the sum of the angles of a triangle is 180°.

A: Proof involves parallel lines and alternate interior angles.

Q13: Find the value of sin30° + cos60°.

A: Value = 1.

Q14: Find the area of a triangle with base 10 cm and height 5 cm.

A: Area = 25 cm².

Q15: If the perimeter of a rectangle is 30 cm and its length is 10 cm, find its width.

A: Width = 5 cm.

Q16: Find the volume of a cylinder with radius 3 cm and height 7 cm.

A: Volume = 198 cm³.

Q17: Solve for x: 3x - 5 = 16.

A: x = 7.

Q18: Find the surface area of a cube with side 4 cm.

A: Surface area = 96 cm².

Q19: Find the median of the following data: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10.

A: Median = 6.

Q20: Find the value of x if 2x + 3 = 11.

A: x = 4.
